**Jam++** is a modern, all-in-one **time management web app** combining **analog and digital clocks** with a full suite of **productivity tools** ‚Äî including Pomodoro sessions, alarms, countdowns, world clocks, and more.  
Built entirely with **HTML, CSS, and JavaScript**, it runs perfectly on **any static host** such as GitHub Pages or Cloudflare Pages.
